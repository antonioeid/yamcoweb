About Us

The company was founded by Mr. Antoine Yammine and Mr. Joseph Yammine in 1990. It was named under Yamco Gaz and had branched out to Yamco Express which it was established in Zgharta, North Lebanon. Not to mention that Nor Gaz is considered antoher branch of Yamco Gaz.

The idea was to fill and distribute gas tanks in a safe and fast environment, while offering the quality needed.

Services

Below we have listed the services that we offer to our clients along with a brief description.

  • Underground Gaz Installation

    We provide polyethylene gas piping systems which are specifically engineered and certified for the underground environment.

  • Industrial Gas Supply

    We are responsible for supplying Industrial Gas (Propane) which is more popular for Hospitals, restaurants and resorts.

  • Maintenance
